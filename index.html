Resume Tips

Expert advice to make your resume stand out

01

Tailor for each job

Customize your resume for every application. Mirror keywords from the job description to pass ATS filters.

02

Quantify achievements

Use numbers wherever possible. "Increased sales by 35%" is far stronger than "improved sales performance".

03

Keep it to one page

Unless you have 10+ years of experience, aim for a single page. Recruiters spend ~7 seconds on first scan.

04

Strong summary first

Your professional summary is prime real estate. Make it punchy, specific, and tailored to the role.

05

Use action verbs

Start bullet points with strong verbs: Led, Built, Designed, Launched, Reduced, Managed, Delivered.

06

Proofread carefully

Typos kill applications. Read it backwards, use spell-check, and have a friend review it too.
